Recurring Events

About Recurring Events

These are events that happen on a regular schedule weekly yoga, monthly mixers, etc. Manage them in the Recurring Events tab of your Google Sheet. They are automatically included every time you export your calendar HTML. Sync from Sheet first to see them here.

Recurring Events from Your Sheet
Sync from Sheet first to load recurring events.
How to manage recurring events
  1. Open your Event Inbox Google Sheet
  2. Click the Recurring Events tab at the bottom
  3. Add rows: Days of week | Time | Event Name | Location | Description | Price | Category | Booking URL
  4. Return here and click Sync from Sheet
  5. When you export the calendar, recurring events are included automatically
Export Calendar HTML

Click "Export Calendar HTML" above to generate updated in-the-city.html code with all your Approved events. Copy it and replace your existing file then upload to WordPress.
