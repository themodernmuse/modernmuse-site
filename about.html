Modern Muse
/ ˈmädərn myo͞oz /
noun · lifestyle · identity
A person who refuses to fit into a single bubble. You are the professional, the artist, the explorer, and the student often all before Wednesday.

The Modern Muse is a commitment to living every day as it comes, fueled by a relentless curiosity for the world around you and a refusal to let a single 24-hour cycle go to waste.

"We don't just find things to do; we find ways to be inspired."
Who We Are

Built for the Multi-faceted.

The Modern Muse exists for those who see every day as a canvas. We're the platform for the people who don't wait for circumstance to live fully who turn a random Tuesday into a memory, a Wednesday morning into a ritual, and a Sunday afternoon into something worth talking about.

We curate the most inspiring moments happening around you from intimate wine tastings and rooftop sunsets to art walks, creative workshops, and the best-kept social hours in your city so you can spend less time searching and more time experiencing.

Our Values

A Note From the Founder

The Art of the Every Day

I've spent my career navigating the complexities of healthcare operations and project management a world where precision, structure, and foresight are the baseline. But I've always believed that the same energy we pour into our professional lives should be used to fuel our personal passions.

The Modern Muse didn't start as a business plan; it started as my way of life.

"I've never been someone who 'lives for the weekend.' To me, a Tuesday evening is just as full of potential as a Saturday night."

Whether it's finding a sunrise yoga session on a rooftop, mastering a new skill in a creative workshop, or discovering a hidden event right in your own backyard, I've always been the one with the running list of "what's next."

I built this platform for the multi-faceted. For those who refuse to fit into a single bubble. For the professionals who are also artists, the parents who are also explorers, and the dreamers who want to be inspired by the cities they call home.

My goal is to take the guesswork out of being active and engaged. I'm bringing my background in seamless implementation to the world of lifestyle and culture, curating the moments that make every day feel like a destination.

We aren't just looking for things to do. We are looking for ways to live.

Let's find your next spark,
Ash B.
Founder, The Modern Muse
